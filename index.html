Privacy Policy for Shadow Company App

Last updated: 2025-11-08

1. Data we collect

When you log in with Facebook we may collect your public_profile, email, and page data (page ids and page access tokens) with your consent. We use these to subscribe your page to our webhook and to process messages sent to your Page.

2. How we use your data

We use page data and messages only to deliver the service (receive messages to your Page and create leads). We do not sell your data.

3. Data retention and deletion

You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at privacy@my-privacy.com. Page tokens are stored securely and can be revoked by you at any time.

4. Contact

If you have any questions, contact privacy@my-privacy.com.
